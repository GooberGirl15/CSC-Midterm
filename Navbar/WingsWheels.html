Wings & Wheels

The Flying Circus Airshow in Bealeton performs every Sunday at 2:30 pm from May thru October. Enjoy the greatest All-American Barnstorming Airshow and meet the greatest Car and Motorcycle owners around! The Flying Circus has arranged these special shows just for you

Rules and other Inforamtion

  1. Vehicle registration from 11 am to 2 pm. Airshow from 2:30 to 4:30 pm.
  2. Voting from People's Choice ballots, Winners announced at 4:30 pm.
  3. Grand prize winner receives the Flying Circus Trophy.
  4. This is a rare photo opportunity for you to pose with your wheels and a antique biplane. Bring your camera!
